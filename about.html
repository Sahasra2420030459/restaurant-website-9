Our Story

About Us

A Journey of Passion and Flavor

Since 2015

Founded with a Dream

Savor & Sage began with a simple dream: to create a place where food becomes an experience. What started as a small family kitchen has grown into one of Mumbai's most beloved dining destinations.

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that great food comes from great ingredients. We partner with local farmers, artisan producers, and sustainable fisheries to bring you the freshest flavors.

Every dish tells a story — of tradition, innovation, and the joy of sharing a meal with loved ones. Our team of passionate chefs crafts each plate with care, creativity, and a deep respect for culinary heritage.
